After filling out all questions, press the "submit answers" button. The application will calculate your chances and redirect you to a page on which you can view this percentage and a detailed breakdown of which questions which influence on the final result, so you can work on these specific issues. The page also provides you, depending on the result of your questions some tips to improve your relationship. These tips are dynamically calculated and are divided as follows: Lower then 33% percent is a low chance to get a divorce. Between 33% and 66% is a medium chance to get a divorce. Higer then 66% is a high chance to get a divorce.
