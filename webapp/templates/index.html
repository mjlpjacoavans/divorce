About this test
With this test you can test whether your relationship can end in divorce.
How does this test work?
This test uses machine learning. This means that a computer program has learned aspects important in divorces and will use this knowledge of previous questionnaires to determine how much your data matches the data of individuals who got divorced. More information about the data the program has learned can be found under the heading: Accuracy and precision.
Dataset
This test is based on collection of questionnaires from divorced and non-divorced people. These questionnaires are. A computer program then analyzed the answers in order to gain useful insights and predict future divorces. When you submit your questionnaire, the program will check whether it can recognize connections in your questionnaire compared to the previously completed questionnaires. The extent to which your answers correspond to the answers of previous persons determines the prediction.
Accuracy and precision
The program uses its previously gained knowledge to make a prediction. Although the model currently has an accuracy of {{round(accuracy*100,1)}}%, we can not guarantee that the outcome will always be corresponds to reality. If the program shows you have a high chance of getting divorced, or in case of any doubt, it is wise to contact a relationship professional.
Data privacy
Your questionnaire submissions are confidential and are not stored on the server. After you finished viewing your prediction results, this result will be destroyed. None of the data you enter will be collected and used to train future models.
